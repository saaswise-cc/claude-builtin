# Venue Comparison — Q3 Offsite

Fictional shortlist, for illustration. In a real project, this is the kind of document Claude Code would produce and update as options come in — not something someone writes by hand.

| Venue | Capacity | Price | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Riverside Conference Center | 30 | $4,200 / 2 days | Closest to downtown, on-site catering available |
| Hillcrest Retreat House | 25 | $3,600 / 2 days | Quieter, 40 min drive, own kitchen (BYO caterer) |
| Union Hall | 40 | $5,100 / 2 days | Largest space, no on-site catering, parking is limited |

**Current lean:** Riverside — best balance of location and included catering, though Hillcrest is worth a second look if the group is closer to 20 than 30.
